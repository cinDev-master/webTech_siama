Siama is the laid-back elegance of home.

Stay at Siama Hotel and leave the rush of urban living behind. This simple haven is a place to mindfully reconnect with one’s peace, spend time with friends and family, and immerse in the rhythms of nature. Welcome Home.

Nestled along the single-street sitio of San Lorenzo in Sorsogon City, in the midst of an old coconut plantation with towering fruiting trees of the region, Siama Hotel hosts 30 bespoke rooms with modern Filipino interiors, a quaint restaurant that anchors its cuisine on the local region’s fare, and a lush garden of tropical flora and fauna sure to tickle the nature-lover’s fancy.
